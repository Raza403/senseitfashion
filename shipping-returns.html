Return & Exchange Policy

US

Our intention is creating products to the highest standard and we hope that you fall in love with your SENSE-IT shirt. If you are unhappy with your purchase for any reason, we will accept returns and issue a full refund to the original form of payment (excluding the shipping cost), so long as the item and all of its packaging are returned completely undamaged. To issue a return we must first be notified via email. Returns MUST be shipped back in the original packaging (including all additional materials), within seven days of product receipt.

Unless otherwise notified, the return shipping cost is the sole responsibility of the customer. Refunds will be made to the original form of payment only, after the product has been received. Please allow 14 business days from the time of receipt, for the item to be processed. If you have any return inquiries, please email info@sense-itfashion.com
